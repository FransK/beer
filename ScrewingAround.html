Here are a few things that you should know about coffee!

  1. Clean water makes all the difference
  2. The right grind is very important. Too course and it'll taste like toilet water. Too fine and it'll taste like swamp water!
  3. Time is key. The longer you brew, the more bitter the coffee tastes. You want to pull out all the sour and sweetness from the bean while only grabbing a touch of the bitter.

    Here's a few things you should know off the bat:

    • India pale ales, (IPAs) have a ton of that hippidy hoppy goodness because they had to travel on long sea voyages from India to get the beer here.
    • Wheat ales tend to have crazy huge heads on 'em! Careful pouring!
    • Lagers are crisp, and easy drinking. Sweeter than pale ales.
